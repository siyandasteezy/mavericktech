Flagship Product

DeepSight is not just a map. It is an intelligent maritime monitoring and intelligence system that combines satellite observation, terrestrial tracking data and analytics to provide improved visibility of vessel activity, abnormal movement and maritime risk across selected areas of interest.

The name DeepSight reflects the purpose of the system: to see deeper than ordinary monitoring tools. In maritime environments, not all activity is visible through one data source. A vessel may transmit its position normally, move slowly, loiter, stop unexpectedly, enter a sensitive area, switch off tracking signals, or operate outside normal patterns. DeepSight is designed to bring these signals together and support smarter maritime awareness.

DeepSight Capabilities

DeepSight gives maritime stakeholders a smarter way to monitor vessel activity, understand risk and make better decisions. By combining space-based observation, terrestrial tracking information and analytics, DeepSight supports improved visibility across coastal and maritime environments where ordinary monitoring may not be enough.

Multi-Source Intelligence

DeepSight combines satellite and terrestrial data sources where available, delivering a richer operating picture than any single source can provide.

Behavioural Analytics

The system is designed to analyse movement patterns, not only display positions — detecting unusual route patterns, stopping behaviour, speed changes and unexpected movement.

Maritime Area Monitoring

Monitoring of selected coastlines, ports, anchorage zones or operating areas — configured around the client's coastline, port, route or infrastructure area.

Satellite-Supported Detection

Use of satellite observation to support wider-area maritime visibility, including comparison between vessel-reported positions and satellite-based observations where applicable.

Dark Vessel Awareness

DeepSight can support investigation of vessels or objects that may not be clearly visible in normal tracking data, identifying possible gaps in the maritime picture.

Risk Scoring & Alerting

Generation of alerts or risk indicators based on movement, location and behaviour rules, enabling clients to respond before incidents occur.

Operational Dashboard

A professional map-based interface for monitoring and decision support — presenting information in a way that supports real operational decisions.

Intelligence Reporting

DeepSight can support summaries, evidence, operational reporting and management-level outputs for operations, risk management and compliance decisions.

Historical Review

Ability to review past vessel activity and movement patterns for analysis and investigation, supporting long-range planning and trend identification.
